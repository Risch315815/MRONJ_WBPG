Medication-Related Osteonecrosis of the Jaw (MRONJ)

本網頁是關於抗骨質吸收藥物使用者在牙科治療後發生藥物相關性顎骨壞死(MRONJ)的風險。

This webpage about risk assessment of anti-resorptive medication users developing MRONJ after dental treatment.

透過收集患者的用藥史和相關病史，我們可以提供個人化衛生教育。

We provide personalized oral hygiene instruction based on the patient's medication history.

請注意：本網站的風險評估結果僅供參考，應根據醫療專業人員的判斷制定治療計畫。

Please note: The risk assessment results from this website are for reference only. Treatment plans should be based on the judgment of healthcare professionals.

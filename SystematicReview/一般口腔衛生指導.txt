一般口腔衛生指導
- 請務必告知所有醫療照護人員（如腫瘤科醫師、內分泌科醫師、牙醫師）您目前或曾使用抗骨吸收藥物。
- 每天至少刷牙兩次，使用軟毛牙刷及含氟牙膏（氟濃度 >1000 ppm），並在刷牙前使用牙線。
- 若牙醫師建議，請使用牙間刷清潔牙齒鄰接面。
- 請每3–6個月定期做牙科檢查與洗牙。
- 若出現牙齦疼痛、腫脹或骨頭外露等情形，請立即通知牙醫師。
- 控制糖尿病、戒菸並保持均衡營養，有助於促進口腔及骨骼健康。
- 請勿自行停用抗骨吸收藥物；如需暫停用藥（藥物假期），應與開立藥物的醫師共同討論並依其建議進行。

半侵入性治療（如根管治療、牙周刮治等）特別指導
- 依照醫師指示服用抗生素與止痛藥，勿自行增減劑量或停藥。

侵入性治療（如拔牙、植牙、補骨手術等）特別指導
-- 手術前（Preoperative）
- 預防性抗生素： 請依牙醫師處方服用，通常於手術前一天開始。
- 請與開立藥物的醫師確認是否需要暫停抗骨吸收藥物（藥物假期）。
-- 傷口照護（Wound care）
- 手術後24小時內冰敷，以減少腫脹。
- 手術後一個星期請避免抽菸、用力吐口水或使用吸管。
- 飲食： 請食用柔軟、溫涼的食物數日，避免過熱或刺激性食物。
- 疼痛與感染控制： 請完整服用醫師開立的抗生素與止痛藥，不要自行停藥。
-- 術後追蹤
- 請於手術後1週回診檢查傷口，之後每2–4週追蹤一次，直到黏膜完全癒合為止。
-- 若出現下列情況，請立即告知牙醫師：
- 疼痛或腫脹持續超過1週未改善。
 -口內骨頭外露或潰瘍8週以上仍未癒合。
 -下巴或嘴唇出現麻木或刺痛感。

General Hygiene instruction
- Inform all your healthcare providers (oncologists, endocrinologists, dentists) about your medication history.
- Brush at least twice daily using a soft-bristled toothbrush and fluoride toothpaste (>1000 ppm), use dental floss before brushing.
- Use interdental brushes if instructed by your dentist.
- Have dental checkup and teeth scaling every 3–6 months.
- Inform your dentist if there is gum pain, swelling, or exposed bone.
- Be sure to control diabetes, stop smoking, and maintain balanced nutrition.
- Do not self-discontinue anti-resorptive medication; any drug holiday should be coordinated with the prescribing physician.

Semi-invasive Treatment specific instruction
- Take antibiotics and analgesics exactly as prescribed.

Invasive Treatment specific instruction
-- Preoperative
- Antibiotic prophylaxis: Follow dentist’s prescription (commonly started 1 day before surgery).
- Confirm with prescribing physician whether temporary suspension of anti-resorptive medication is appropriate.
-- Wound care
- Apply cold compress in the first 24 hours to minimize swelling.
- Do not smoke, spit forcefully, or use straws for the first week.
- Diet: Soft, lukewarm foods for several days.
- Pain and infection control: Complete the full course of antibiotics and analgesics as prescribed.
-- Follow-up
- Schedule wound check at 1 week, then every 2–4 weeks until mucosal healing is complete.
-- Inform your dentist if:
- Persistent pain or swelling beyond one week
- Exposed bone or ulcer that doesn’t heal after 8 weeks
- Numbness or tingling in jaw/lip area

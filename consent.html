Please read and agree to the following before using the MRONJ Risk Assessment Tool

個人資料收集聲明
Personal Data Collection Statement
本風險評估工具僅用於評估您的MRONJ（藥物相關性顎骨壞死）風險。我們將收集以下資訊：
  • 基本個人資料（姓名、年齡、性別等）
  • 身高、體重資料
  • 骨質疏鬆或癌症相關藥物使用史
  • 相關疾病史和治療史
所有資料僅用於風險評估，僅會暫時儲存於您的個人裝置，也不會用於其他目的。
This risk assessment tool is only used to evaluate your risk of MRONJ (Medication-Related Osteonecrosis of the Jaw). We will collect the following information:
  • Basic personal data (name, age, gender, etc.)
  • Height and weight data
  • History of osteoporosis or cancer-related medication use
  • Relevant medical history and treatment history
All data is used only for risk assessment, and will only be temporarily saved on your device, and will not be used for other purposes.
風險評估免責聲明
Risk Assessment Disclaimer
本工具提供的風險評估只是參考建議，不構成醫療診斷或替代專業醫療意見。最終治療決策應由您與您的醫療團隊共同做出。
使用本工具並不建立醫患關係，也不免除您的醫療團隊進行全面評估的責任。
The risk assessment provided by this tool is only a reference suggestion and does not constitute a medical diagnosis or substitute for professional medical advice. Final treatment decisions should be made by you and your healthcare team.
Using this tool does not establish a doctor-patient relationship, nor does it exempt your healthcare team from the responsibility of conducting a comprehensive assessment.

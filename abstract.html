量化抗骨吸收藥物治療患者侵入性牙科手術前後MRONJ風險：基於統合分析之網路平台
Quantifying MRONJ Risk Before and After Invasive Dental Procedures in Antiresorptive Medication-treated Patients: A Meta-analysis-informed Web Platform
徐振傑1, 袁行修2, 汪硯雲3
Chen-Chieh Hsu1, Shyng-Shiou Yuan2, Yen-Yun Wang3
1 理想牙醫診所，高雄，台灣
2 高雄醫學大學醫學院醫學研究所，高雄，台灣
3 高雄醫學大學口腔醫學院牙醫學系，高雄，台灣
1 Dental Department, Ideal Dental Clinic, Kaohsiung, Taiwan
2 Graduate Institute of Medicine, College of Medicine, Kaohsiung Medical University, Kaohsiung, Taiwan
3 School of Dentistry, College of Dental Medicine, Kaohsiung Medical University, Kaohsiung, Taiwan
前言
Introduction
藥物相關性顎骨失活(MRONJ)雖然不常見，但持續的疼痛和重複的介入治療可能破壞醫病信任關係。許多接受抗骨吸收治療的患者不知道侵入性牙科治療會顯著增加風險，而牙醫師面臨時間限制來驗證藥物並提供客製化諮詢。需要一個簡潔、基於證據的平台來記錄抗骨吸收暴露並傳達個人化、針對特定程序的風險。
Medication-related osteonecrosis of the jaw (MRONJ) is uncommon, yet prolonged pain and repeat interventions can undermine patient–clinician trust. Many patients receiving antiresorptive therapy are unaware that invasive dental treatment markedly increases risk, and dentists face time constraints in verifying medications and providing tailored counseling. A concise, evidence-based platform that records antiresorptive exposure and communicates individualized, procedure-specific risk is needed.
目標
Objective
綜合分析按適應症、藥物、給藥途徑和牙科治療侵入性分層的MRONJ發生率。這些數據將支持一個基於網路的個人化風險評估平台，用於患者教育。
To synthesize pooled MRONJ incidence by indication (osteoporosis, cancer), drug class (bisphosphonate, denosumab, romosozumab), administration route (oral, IV/SC), and invasiveness of dental treatment. These data would support a web-based, personalized risk-evaluation platform for patient education.
方法
Method
我們進行了MRONJ發生率的統合分析，按以下因素進行分層：

• 適應症   - 骨質疏鬆症   - 癌症
• 藥物類別   - 雙磷酸鹽   - Denosumab   - Romosozumab
• 給藥途徑   - 口服   - 靜脈/皮下
• 侵入性牙科治療之有無
同質性層使用固定效應模型進行合併；異質性或稀疏層以事件加權比例進行總結。這些數據填充了平台的風險顯示和雙語患者手冊。
We performed a meta-analysis of MRONJ incidence stratified by the following factors:

• Indication   - Osteoporosis   - Cancer
• Drug class   - Bisphosphonate   - Denosumab   - Romosozumab
• Administration route   - Oral   - IV/SC
• Presence of invasive dental treatment
Homogeneous strata were pooled with fixed-effects models; heterogeneous or sparse strata were summarized as event-weighted proportions. These data populate the platform's risk display and bilingual patient handouts.
結果
Results
在兩種適應症中，發生率從無藥物、僅藥物、到藥物加侵入性牙科治療呈階梯式增加：

• 骨質疏鬆症   - 無藥物：0.04%   - 僅藥物：0.20%   - 藥物 + 侵入性牙科治療：1.48%
• 癌症   - 無藥物：0.09%   - 僅藥物：1.09%   - 藥物 + 侵入性牙科治療：9.92%
各藥物類別的發生率大致一致，除了三個僅有單一研究數據的亞組需要謹慎解釋。這些梯度已嵌入平台的風險評估系統中。
Across both indications, incidence increased stepwise from no medication, to medication only, to medication with invasive dental treatment:

• Osteoporosis   - No medication: 0.04%   - Medication only: 0.20%   - Medication + invasive dental treatment: 1.48%
• Cancer   - No medication: 0.09%   - Medication only: 1.09%   - Medication + invasive dental treatment: 9.92%
Rates were broadly consistent across drug classes, with the exception of three single-study subgroups, interpreted cautiously. These gradients are embedded in the platform's risk-evaluation system.
結論
Conclusion
當接受抗骨吸收藥物治療的患者進行侵入性牙科治療時，MRONJ風險大約增加一個數量級，這在各種適應症和藥物類別中都是如此。將這些合併數據嵌入椅旁網路工具中，能夠快速識別藥物並提供個人化諮詢，支持知情同意和滿意的治療。
MRONJ risk rises by approximately an order of magnitude when invasive dental treatment is performed in patients exposed to antiresorptive medications, across indications and drug classes. Embedding these pooled figures in a chair-side web tool enables rapid medication identification and personalized counseling, supporting informed consent and satisfied treatment.

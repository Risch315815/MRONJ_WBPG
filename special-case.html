若患者曾罹患轉移至顎骨之癌症或接受頭頸部放射治療，顎骨壞死可能由不同於MRONJ的機制所導致，且風險較高，評估方式將有所不同。
請諮詢您的醫師以取得更精確的評估。

Note: If the patient has had cancer that metastasis to the jaw or received radiotherapy of the head and neck region, osteonecrosis of the jaw may occur through different mechanisms than MRONJ, with a higher risk.
Please consult your doctor for a more accurate assessment.
